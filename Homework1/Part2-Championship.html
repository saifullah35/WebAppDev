Championships






The Lakers have won 17 NBA championships. They have NBA legends play for them such as Kobe Bryant, Magic Johnson, Kareem-Abdul Jabbar, and many more. They have won championships in 1949, 1950, 1952, 1953, 1954, 1972, 1980, 1982, 1985, 1987, 1988, 2000, 2001, 2002, 2009, 2010, and 2020.They are one of the most successful NBA teams of all time advanced and are tied with the Boston Celtics with the most championships of all time. They are currently struggling right now but hopefully will be able to win more rings in the future.
